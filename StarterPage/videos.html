More Videos on Mothman

The legend of Mothman has appeared across several popular paranormal storytelling outlets, such as The History Channel's Ancient Aliens.


The cryptid captures the public's fascination as folklore and science fiction tend to blur.


Many popular online paranormal investigators, such as Ryan Bergara and Shane Madej from Buzzfeed Unsolved travel to Point Pleasant, West Virginia to look for the monster.


Venturing further into pop culture, Mothman became the main subject of the 2002 horror The Mothman Prophecies.


Many believers tend to link the Silver Bridge collapse of 1967 with the appearances of Mothman as the semi-frequent reports of seeing the monster trickled to a stop after the disaster.
